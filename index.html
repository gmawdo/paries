Building Proof Systems for Trust & Transparency

Our core framework named Towards Zero focuses on proof-of-origin and proof-of-computation methodologies. We develop systems that can mathematically prove that a dataset was derived from a known source and that any transformations applied to it were performed according to defined rules. These proofs have applications across supply chains, compliance, and secure data workflows, and with particular reference to the regulatory challenges posed by AI.

AI workloads demand secure, high-performance data infrastructure capable of handling massive models and ensuring verifiable computation integrity. Towards Zero provides the platform supporting provable AI data integrity, fault-tolerant storage, and secure routing‚Äîall backed by zero-knowledge cryptography and a formally verified OS foundation.

Digital Concrete for AI Blueprint

Virtual-world application of Towards Zero

At its core, the blueprint integrates a FUSE-based filesystem with seL4 for formally verified execution, coupled with a sharded object store that ensures efficient parallel retrieval of large AI models.

ZKP-based authentication and SCION route proofs guarantee that only authorised entities access data and that every file follows a verifiable, expected network path.

Data is protected through STARK proofs, Byzantine fault tolerance, and Merkle-based metadata tracking, ensuring that AI models and their outputs remain immutable and traceable. Each model and inference is cryptographically fingerprinted, embedding integrity directly into the AI pipeline.

By combining secure storage, scalable retrieval, and transparent verification, Towards Zero redefines how AI data is stored, accessed, and proven in a trustless world.

How Our Work Relates to Blockchain

As noted cryptographer Bruce Schneier points out, blockchain is fundamentally a decentralised transaction system that leverages robust, industry-standard cryptography combined with various consensus approaches. This technological foundation makes blockchain an excellent solution for the privacy preserving public storage of data fingerprints and proofs.

While we don't position ourselves as a "blockchain company", we recognise blockchain's value as a transparent, immutable public ledger. When combined with our Towards Zero framework, blockchain serves as an ideal platform for:

This approach allows us to leverage blockchain's strengths in transparency and immutability while maintaining data privacy and computational efficiency in our core systems.
