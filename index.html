Paries – The Power of sealed Provenance & Proof

Who We Are

Paries develops cryptographic proof systems that ensure integrity, traceability, and transparency in both physical and digital workflows. We create technologies that attest to the origin of data, validate transformations, and provide verifiable proofs without exposing sensitive information.

Our Technology

Building Proof Systems for Trust & Transparency

Our core research focuses on proof-of-origin and proof-of-computation methodologies. We develop systems that can mathematically prove that a dataset was derived from a known source and that any transformations applied to it were performed according to defined rules. These proofs have applications across supply chains, compliance, and secure data workflows.

Use Cases

Eden Consignment Tracking – A Proof of Shipment

Real-world application of our proof technology.

Eden Consignment Tracking applies our principles of verifiable provenance to global logistics. The platform provides real-time visibility into shipments, linking each consignment to detailed carrier data and structured manifests.

Cambridge University Press & Assessment is among our valued customers, relying on Eden to track their exam materials worldwide with precision.
